Introduction Week for
MOT Students 2023

19 to 21 September 2023, Delft University of Technology

Registration Form

Join Us for Introduction Days!

Connect, Learn, and Have Fun: Get to know your fellow students, discover more about The Netherlands, and enjoy some faculty-free fun! This year, we have prepared an exciting three-day program just for you.

Delicious Meals Provided: We have got you covered with lunch and a BBQ during the three days. Please inform us of any dietary requirements, so we can cater to your needs. Events with food will be clearly marked, giving you the flexibility to grab a bite on your own or bring your own snacks.

Important Registration: DO REGISTER and let us know about your dietary preferences, if applicable, via the registration form. The deadline is Sunday, September 17, 2023, at 23:59.

Calling All First-Year MOT Students: This invitation is for you! We look forward to seeing each one of you next week.

Let's make these Introduction Days memorable together!

Delft University of Technology

Discover the Secrets of TU Delft!

Explore the hidden gems of TU Delft as we navigate through the campus, led by an expert guide who will reveal fascinating insights about the places we'll visit during the tour. Don't miss this unique opportunity to uncover the secrets of one of the world's leading universities!

16.30 – 18.30

Wrap Up Day One with Fun and BBQ at Delftse Hout!
Delftse Hout 

Enjoy a tasty BBQ by the calm lake and have fun with fellow students as we play group games like ball games, frisbees, and more! Feel free to bring your own games and equipment to make the experience even more enjoyable.

9:15

Meeting at Delft Centraal
Delft Centraal 

On the second day, we will be heading outside Delft. So, we will all gather at Delft Centraal, and then, take the train to The Hague as a group.

9:50 - 12:15

Explore History at The Hague Historical Museum!
Haags Historisch Museum 

Discover the extensive collection of 7,500 fascinating objects. Come and delve into the story of The Hague like never before. Explore centuries of heritage through art and artifacts. Don't miss this enlightening experience!

12:15 - 16:00

Free Time

After our museum visit, we will enjoy some free time in the city before taking a tram to Scheveningen. There, we can take a beach walk, explore De Pier, and optionally experience the observation wheel (additional cost applies). Join us for this seaside adventure on our next activity!

16:00 - 18:30

Seaside Fun in Scheveningen!
Solbeach 

Join us for snacks and refreshing drinks with a view of the sea in Scheveningen. Get active and join in the fun with beach volleyball and other exciting activities. Remember to wear comfortable clothes, and if you would like to sit on the sand, bring a towel! Come prepared for an afternoon of relaxation, games, and delicious treats by the beach. We can't wait to share this fantastic time all together!

9:00

Meeting at Delft Centraal
Delft Centraal 

On our last day, we are going to Rotterdam, a bigger city near Delft. We will meet at Delft Centraal, take the train to Rotterdam together, and then walk to the museums.

10:00 - 12:30

Explore the Maritime Past and Present at the Maritime Museum!
Maritiem Museum 

Step into the Maritime Museum Rotterdam and dive into the interesting world of shipping, uncovering its enormous impact on our daily lives. "Go on a journey with us through the maritime past and present and listen to the stories or join in some numerous activities" (Maritime Museum). Join us for an unforgettable maritime experience where history and adventure meet!

12:30 - 14:30

Discover Nature's Wonders at the Natural History Museum!
Natuurhistorisch Museum Rotterdam 

Step into the world of nature at the Natural History Museum Rotterdam, a place that's been around since 1927. The collection, with about 400,000 samples, let you explore the natural world from around the globe. Join us for an exciting journey through nature's past and present. Come and explore the fascinating collection that reveals the secrets of the natural world!

It is recommended to visit at least one of the two museums!
14:30 - 15:30

Free Time

After the museums, we will have some free time to go for lunch in the centre of Rotterdam (not on the budget) and explore the city before our final activity.

16:00 - 19:00
